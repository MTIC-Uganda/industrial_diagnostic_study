## 5. Things that are findings, not gaps

Worth stating plainly in the reports rather than treating as missing data:
- **Copper:** almost no domestic processing (Kilembe dormant; revival deal Mar 2025).
- **Steel:** 80–90% of demand imported despite Tembo/Devki investment.
- **Fertilizer:** Sukulu plant stalled (shut 2021, halted again 2023) — not the success the 2018/2014 sources imply.
- **Flat steel, plastics, cement clinker:** Uganda a net importer — the import-substitution case.
