DATA ENTRY GUIDE

What is this guide?

The MTIC dashboard at tinyurl.com/28lxntmc shows live data — KPI indicators, value chain snapshots, factory locations, and 2040 projections. All of that data lives in a database called PocketBase. This guide teaches you how to log in and update records whenever figures change.

You do not need to write any code. PocketBase has a simple spreadsheet-like interface in your browser. Click Take a Tour above (or below) to get a step-by-step walkthrough.

🔗 Open PocketBase Admin →
📦
The Three Data Tables
PocketBase holds three collections (tables). Each maps to a section of the dashboard.

value_chains

The 9 priority chains: trade figures, Uganda position, 2040 targets, strategic priority, map description, status narrative, key companies, constraints, and projections.

9 records

facilities

All manufacturing facilities plotted on the industry locations map: name, GPS coordinates, capacity, employees, ownership, products exported.

89 records

kpi_indicators

The six headline KPI cards at the top of the dashboard: GDP, Per Capita Income, Manufacturing Share, Capacity Utilisation, Exports, Employment.

3
Edit a Value Chain Record
Click the edit icon on a chain row. A form opens on the right side. Update the fields that have changed — for example new trade figures — then click Save.
http://89.167.121.193:8090/_/#/collections/value_chains/edit/abc123
value_chains / Iron & Steel
Only edit the fields that have actually changed. Leave all other fields exactly as they are — the dashboard reads every field.
4
Update a KPI Indicator
Click kpi_indicators in the sidebar. Click the record you want to update (e.g. "gdp"). Change current_value and current_pct to reflect the latest figures. Click Save.
http://89.167.121.193:8090/_/#/collections/kpi_indicators/edit/def456
kpi_indicators / GDP (Total)
current_pct is a whole number (0–100) representing how far Uganda is towards the tenfold target. Example: if GDP is USD 53B and the tenfold target is USD 500B, then current_pct = 11.
5
Add a New Manufacturing Facility
Click facilities in the sidebar, then click + New record. Fill in the form below and click Save. The factory will appear on the Industry Locations map immediately.
http://89.167.121.193:8090/_/#/collections/facilities/new
facilities / New Record
Getting GPS coordinates: Go to maps.google.com, right-click the factory location, and click the coordinates at the top of the menu. Paste them into lat and lng (lat first, lng second).
6
After Saving — Updating the Live Dashboard
Your changes are saved in PocketBase immediately. The live dashboard at tinyurl.com/28lxntmc reads from PocketBase every time CI deploys. To push your changes live:
Option A — Self-serve
  1. Open GitHub Actions
  2. Click Run workflow on the Deploy workflow
  3. Wait ~3 minutes for it to finish
  4. Refresh the dashboard — data is updated
Option B — Ask the team
  1. WhatsApp Solomon or Hillary
  2. Tell them you've updated data in PocketBase
  3. They will trigger a deployment
  4. Dashboard is updated within a few minutes
The technical team is working on making the dashboard auto-refresh from PocketBase without a manual deployment step. Until then, use Option A or B above.
